Privacy Policy

Snake Bytes — Last updated: April 5, 2026

Overview

Snake Bytes is a mobile game developed by an independent developer. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains what data the app does (and does not) collect.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

Snake Bytes does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or any third-party data collection services. The app does not require an account to play.

Data Stored on Your Device

Snake Bytes saves your game progress locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This includes:

  • Game progress (current level, scores, coins)
  • Your settings preferences (music, sound effects, haptics, color palette)
  • Shop purchases (unlocked snake skins)
  • Mission progress

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Game Center

Snake Bytes supports Apple Game Center for achievements, leaderboards, and multiplayer matchmaking. Game Center is managed entirely by Apple. If you choose to sign in to Game Center, your Game Center display name and scores are handled according to Apple's Privacy Policy. Game Center participation is optional and not required to play single-player modes.

Multiplayer

Snake Race mode uses Apple's Game Center real-time matchmaking to connect players. No personal data is shared between players beyond what Game Center provides (display name). Match data is transient and not stored on any server.

Third-Party Services

Snake Bytes does not integrate any third-party advertising networks or analytics platforms. Game Center is the only external service, provided by Apple and governed by Apple's own privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

Snake Bytes does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no advertising and no social features that collect user information.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
