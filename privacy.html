Privacy Policy

Snake Bumper — Last updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

Snake Bumper is a snake arcade game developed by an independent developer. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains what data the app does (and does not) collect.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

Snake Bumper does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or any third-party data collection services. The app does not require an account to play.

Data Stored on Your Device

Snake Bumper saves your game progress locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This includes:

  • Game progress (high scores, levels)
  • Your settings preferences (music, sound effects, haptics)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Game Center

Snake Bumper supports Apple Game Center for achievements and leaderboards. Game Center is managed entirely by Apple. If you choose to sign in to Game Center, your Game Center display name and scores are handled according to Apple's Privacy Policy. Game Center participation is optional and not required to play.

Third-Party Services

Snake Bumper does not integrate any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

Snake Bumper does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no advertising, no in-app purchases that require personal data, and no social features that collect user information.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
